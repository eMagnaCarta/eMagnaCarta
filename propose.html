Please contribute your ideas on process, substance and the key questions to be addressed as part of developing an eMagnaCarta.

The following online processes are being explored as potential tools to catalyze and support contribution to citizens' engagement in co-drafting an eMagnaCarta. To communicate about logistics and planning, please check out the resources on our project participation page. To help evaluate tools for effective, valid and efficient mass-scale collaborate on the actual drafting and agreement of an eMagnaCarta, please try out the applications and services below and share your suggestions and ideas for other tools that may be good candidates for the task.

The questions.dev.berkmancenter.org/list/MagnaCarta application uses a Socratic process and has been contributed by Harvard Law School Professor Charlie Nesson. The site is also available at tinyurl.com/qaqmbes.
